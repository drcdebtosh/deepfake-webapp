About This Project

Our Deepfake Detection project leverages cutting-edge machine learning techniques to identify manipulated media content.
Using a combination of 3D convolutional neural networks (CNNs) and facial landmark analysis,
it detects alterations in video footage, distinguishing real from deepfake with high accuracy.
This technology is designed to help combat misinformation and ensure the authenticity of digital media.
